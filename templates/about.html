About the Author

Connor Tao

Hi, I’m Connor — an actuarial science major at UC Santa Barbara who spends way too much time at K1 Speed.

I built this app because I wanted a clean, simple way to track lap times, analyze performance, and see improvement over time. What started as a small personal project grew into a full data dashboard with charts, filters, import tools, and track pages. I love racing, stats, and building things that make life easier, and this app brings all those interests together.
