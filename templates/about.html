Built for racers, by a racer

This project started as a personal tracker and evolved into a full lap-time lab. Every feature — from parsing emails to slicing lap data — was shaped by weekends at the track and hours obsessing over splits.

  • Instant lap imports from race emails
  • Clean charts to visualize progress over time
  • Track-specific views and leaderboards
  • Filters that surface your best laps and sessions

The philosophy

Simple enough to use after a heat, detailed enough to help you shave tenths. The design focuses on clarity, quick insights, and staying out of the way while you race and iterate.
